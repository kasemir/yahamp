Why?

YAHAMP is Yet Another HAM Program that grew out of experimenting with Eclipse.

No hard-core contesting tool but mostly meant to keep a log, help to remember who you've contacted before, keep track of who you QSL'ed, get an idea where the other station is located:

  • Logbook based on MySQL
  • Address lookup from www.qrz.com, supporting the XML subscription mode
  • Frequency/mode readout for Icom and Yaesu rigs
  • Display of previous QSOs with a station
  • Grayline map displays location of you and selected contact
  • Basic distance calculation

Most important, it is still my Eclipse (E4) playground.
